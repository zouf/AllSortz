AllSorts Help

How it Works

AllSortz allows you to leave your footprint in the business world with stimulating commentary and conversation about the places you have been and would like to go. With AllSortz you can find places and activities that you are interested in and share that experience with others.

Businesses

Our business listings grow each day as more locations are found and shared on AllSortz. Each business can be sorted, described, and discussed, giving users an idea of what might be interesting to visit.

Sorts

Sorts serve to categorize and describe businesses, giving you a general idea of how others might view them. They guide your searches and group businesses together under a common label that lets you discuss and compare between them.

Questions

A set of yes/no questions can be found on all business profile pages. These questions provide you with more information about the business in a quick and easy-to-read fashion, giving you a fast summary of some important aspects of the business.

Traits

These can be found on your user profile. They describe your personal preferences: you can rank each trait in its importance to you, with 1 indicating little or no interest and 3 indicating a lot of interest. This will help guide future suggestions made for you.

Bugs and problems

AllSortz is currently still in its developmental phase. As you use the site, there may be various problems that you encounter. Should this happen, please contact us with a description of the bug and when you encountered it. We appreciate your help!
